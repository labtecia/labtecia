AIIS Team

Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems

Mission

To advance research and innovation in Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems, our team is dedicated to developing intelligent, adaptive, and data-driven solutions that address real-world challenges across multiple domains including software engineering, signal and image processing, decision support systems, and smart technologies.

Research Focus

  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning: Designing and developing advanced ML/DL models for prediction, classification, optimization, and intelligent decision-making,
  • Intelligent Systems and Data Analysis: Building intelligent systems capable of learning, reasoning, and adapting to complex environments and large-scale data,
  • Computer Vision and Signal Processing: Applying AI techniques in image analysis, signal processing, pattern recognition, and multimedia understanding,
  • Natural Language Processing and Knowledge Extraction: Developing NLP-based approaches for text analysis, information retrieval, and human-computer interaction,
  • AI Applications in Software Engineering: Exploring the use of AI methods for software quality assessment, automation, maintenance, and intelligent development tools.
