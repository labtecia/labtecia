SPDS Team

Smart Distributed and Pervasive Systems

Mission

The Smart Distributed and Pervasive Systems (SDPS) Team, conducts cutting edge research that focuses on challenges surrounding the development of systems combining Cloud Computing and the Internet of Things (IoT). In order to enable the convergence of smart objects, big data and Clouds, and create new frameworks able to offer services for detecting, controlling and processing big data. Cloud platforms are used to store data, use it for the creation of “smart environments”, and develop advanced models which can help to identify, monitor, optimize, learn and process our environment. The IOT will evolve towards a cognitive IOT model in which objects have the ability to learn and understand the physical and the social world. Pattern mining algorithms, machine learning methods and artificial intelligence techniques will be designed and executed on the Cloud in a distributed and elastic fashion to automate decision making processes.

Research Focus

  • Smart City: The Smart Cities research carries out applied research and covers various facets of the system development life cycle for smart city solutions. Our main areas of interest include city data governance and analytics, security and privacy, piloting and evaluation. More specifically, we apply distributed technologies including but not limited to Cloud, distributed ledgers, IoTs, generative AI for smart city solutions,
  • Health care: Health care is a very interesting domain. In this research, we are interested in the processing of large quantities of medical images using distributed systems,
  • Industrial Internet of Things (IIOT): The industrial internet of things (IIoT) refers to the extension and use of the internet of things (IoT) in industrial sectors and applications. With a strong focus on machine-to-machine (M2M) communication, big data, and machine learning, the IIoT enables industries and enterprises to have better efficiency and reliability in their operations.

Dr Meddeber Meriem

Team head

Meddeber Meriem is a teacher/researcher at the University of Mustapha Stambouli of Mascara, Algeria. She received her engineer diploma and post-graduate degree (Magister) in computer science, respectively in 2006 and 2008 from the university of Mustapha Stambouli of Mascara. She is currently an associate professor in Mustapha Stambouli university and the team leader of Smart Distributed and Pervasive Systems (SDPS) team, associated to the Technological Laboratory In Artificial Intelligence And Food Safety (LABTECAI). Actually working on: Large Scale Distributed Systems, Tasks Scheduling in Distributed Systems, Data Consistency in distributed Systems, Deep learning, Machine learning, IoT security, and intrusion detection systems.
