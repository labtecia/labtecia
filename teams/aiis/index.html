AIIS Team

Using AI Techniques in Information Security

Mission

To pioneer innovative solutions in information security by leveraging the power of artificial intelligence. Our team is dedicated to developing advanced techniques to protect sensitive data and digital systems from emerging threats..

Research Focus

  • AI-Driven Threat Detection: Developing intelligent systems to proactively identify and respond to cyberattacks,
  • Machine/Deep Learning for Vulnerability Assessment: Utilizing ML/DL algorithms to automate vulnerability detection and risk assessment,
  • Natural Language Processing for Security Analysis: Employing NLP techniques to analyze security logs, reports, and threat intelligence,
