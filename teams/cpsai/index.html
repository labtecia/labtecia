CPSAI Team

Cyber-Physical Systems & Artificial Intelligence

Mission

The mission of the "Cyber-Physical Systems & Artificial Intelligence" team is to design, develop, and optimize Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) solutions to address complex challenges. We focus on integrating AI with embedded systems to transform the interactions between the physical and digital worlds.

Our objectives include:
  • Promoting innovation in the design of intelligent and interconnected systems,
  • Enhancing the reliability and energy efficiency of CPS across various sectors,
  • Applying advanced technologies to meet strategic needs in health, food security, Industry 4.0, and smart infrastructures

Research Focus

The team focuses on the following key areas:

  • Machine Learning in Embedded & Cyber-Physical Systems: Developing algorithms tailored for real-time, energy-efficient embedded systems and CPS,
  • Hardware/Software/Physical Systems Co-design: Innovating integrated methodologies to optimize performance and reduce power consumption,
  • Security in Embedded & Cyber-Physical Systems: Addressing cybersecurity challenges to ensure robust and secure operations,
  • Precision Agriculture: Developing CPS solutions for smart water quality monitoring, wastewater monitoring, water distribution management, and optimization of fertilizer and irrigation systems,
  • Autonomous Systems: Exploring CPS applications in autonomous vehicles and neuromorphic architectures for advanced AI deployment.

Our research bridges theoretical advancements and practical applications, with a focus on sectors such as agriculture, water management, healthcare, health, and transportation, leveraging cutting-edge CPS and AI technologies.
